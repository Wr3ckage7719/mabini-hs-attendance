How to Send Notifications
  1. Choose the notification type (Info, Warning, Success, or Danger)
  2. Enter a clear title and message
  3. Select who should receive it (All students, specific grade, section, or individual)
  4. Review the recipient count and click Send

Note: Notifications will appear in students' dashboards when they log in.
